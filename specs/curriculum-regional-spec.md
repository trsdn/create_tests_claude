## 1. German Education System

### 1.1 Bundesländer (Federal States)

**16 Bundesländer with distinct curricula:**

| Bundesland | Curriculum Framework | Key Features |
|------------|---------------------|--------------|
| Bayern (Bavaria) | Lehrplan PLUS | Competency-based, modern approach |
| Nordrhein-Westfalen (NRW) | Kernlehrpläne | Comprehensive coverage, G9 standard |
| Baden-Württemberg | Bildungspläne | Strong focus on Realschule |
| Hessen | Kerncurriculum | Modular approach |
| Niedersachsen | Kerncurriculum | Flexible pathways |
| Berlin | Rahmenlehrplan | Combined with Brandenburg |
| Hamburg | Bildungspläne | Competency-oriented |
| Sachsen | Lehrplan | Traditional structure |
| *Others* | Various frameworks | State-specific approaches |

### 1.2 School Types

#### Grundschule (Primary School)
- **Duration:** Grades 1-4 (or 1-6 in Berlin/Brandenburg)
- **Age:** 6-10 years
- **Subjects:** Deutsch, Mathematik, Sachunterricht, Kunst, Musik, Sport
- **Assessment:** Primarily formative, descriptive feedback
- **Transition:** Recommendation for secondary school type

#### Hauptschule
- **Purpose:** Basic education, vocational preparation
- **Duration:** Grades 5-9/10
- **Age:** 10-16 years
- **Difficulty:** Foundational, practical
- **Question Complexity:** Basic knowledge and comprehension
- **Outcome:** Hauptschulabschluss

#### Realschule
- **Purpose:** Intermediate education, practical focus
- **Duration:** Grades 5-10
- **Age:** 10-16 years
- **Difficulty:** Moderate, application-oriented
- **Question Complexity:** Understanding and application level
- **Outcome:** Mittlere Reife (intermediate school certificate)

#### Gymnasium
- **Purpose:** Academic preparation for university (Abitur)
- **Duration:** Grades 5-12 (G8) or 5-13 (G9)
- **Age:** 10-18/19 years
- **Difficulty:** Advanced, theoretical focus
- **Question Complexity:** Higher-order thinking, analysis, synthesis
- **Languages:** Multiple foreign languages required
- **Upper Grades:** Leistungskurse (advanced) and Grundkurse (basic)

#### Gesamtschule (Comprehensive School)
- **Purpose:** Combines all tracks
- **Duration:** Grades 5-10 or 5-13
- **Age:** 10-19 years
- **Difficulty:** Differentiated by course level
- **Question Complexity:** Varies by student track

### 1.3 Klassenstufe (Grade Level) System

**Klassenstufe 1-4 (Grundschule):**
- Foundation building
- Basic literacy and numeracy
- Age: 6-10 years

**Klassenstufe 5-6 (Orientierungsstufe):**
- Transition from primary
- Introduction to new subjects
- Age: 10-12 years

**Klassenstufe 7-10 (Mittelstufe):**
- Subject depth increases
- Preparation for qualification exams
- Age: 12-16 years

**Klassenstufe 11-13 (Oberstufe/Sekundarstufe II):**
- Specialization in Leistungskurse
- Abitur preparation
- Age: 16-19 years

### 1.4 Subject-Specific Standards

#### Mathematics (Mathematik)

**Klassenstufe 5 (Gymnasium, Bayern):**
- Natürliche Zahlen (Natural numbers)
- Grundrechenarten (Basic arithmetic)
- Geometrische Grundbegriffe (Basic geometry)
- Größen und Messen (Measurements)

**Klassenstufe 8 (Gymnasium, NRW):**
- Lineare Funktionen (Linear functions)
- Terme und Gleichungen (Terms and equations)
- Prozentrechnung (Percentage calculation)
- Körperberechnung (3D geometry)

**Klassenstufe 11 (Gymnasium, Baden-Württemberg):**
- Analysis (Differential calculus)
- Analytische Geometrie (Analytical geometry)
- Stochastik (Statistics and probability)

#### Science (Naturwissenschaften)

**Biologie Klassenstufe 7:**
- Ökosysteme (Ecosystems)
- Zellbiologie (Cell biology)
- Evolution (Evolution basics)
- Humanbiologie (Human biology)

**Physik Klassenstufe 9 (Realschule):**
- Mechanik (Mechanics)
- Elektrizitätslehre (Electricity)
- Wärmelehre (Thermodynamics)
- Energie (Energy)

**Chemie Klassenstufe 10 (Gymnasium):**
- Atombau und Periodensystem (Atomic structure)
- Chemische Bindungen (Chemical bonds)
- Säuren und Basen (Acids and bases)
- Organische Chemie (Organic chemistry intro)

#### Languages (Sprachen)

**Deutsch Klassenstufe 6:**
- Textverständnis (Reading comprehension)
- Grammatik (Grammar: cases, tenses)
- Aufsatz (Essay writing)
- Literatur (Literature analysis)

**Englisch Klassenstufe 8 (Gymnasium):**
- CEFR Level: A2-B1
- Grammar: Tenses, conditionals, passive voice
- Vocabulary: 2000-2500 words
- Skills: Reading, writing, listening, speaking

## 3. UK Education System

### 3.1 Structure

**Primary School:** Years 1-6 (Ages 5-11)  
**Secondary School:** Years 7-11 (Ages 11-16)  
**Sixth Form/College:** Years 12-13 (Ages 16-18)

### 3.2 National Curriculum - Key Stages

**Key Stage 1:** Years 1-2 (Ages 5-7)  
**Key Stage 2:** Years 3-6 (Ages 7-11)  
**Key Stage 3:** Years 7-9 (Ages 11-14)  
**Key Stage 4:** Years 10-11 (Ages 14-16) - GCSE preparation

### 3.3 Mathematics - Key Stage 3

**Topics:**
- Algebra: Solve linear equations
- Number: Fractions, decimals, percentages
- Geometry: Properties of shapes
- Statistics: Interpret data

### 3.4 GCSE System

**Foundation Tier:** Grades 1-5  
**Higher Tier:** Grades 4-9

## 5. Regional Adaptation Guidelines

### 5.1 Language Considerations

**German Tests:**
- Use formal "Sie" or informal "du" based on grade level
- Employ correct German mathematical terminology
- Follow German punctuation and grammar rules
- Use metric system exclusively
- Include Umlaute (ä, ö, ü) and ß correctly

**Cultural Adaptation:**
- Use culturally relevant examples (European football for German students)
- Reference local geography and history
- Adapt names to reflect local demographics
- Use region-specific currencies and measurements
- Consider local holidays and seasonal references

### 5.2 Terminology Mapping

**Mathematics (English → German):**
- Addition → Addition
- Subtraction → Subtraktion
- Multiplication → Multiplikation
- Division → Division
- Equation → Gleichung
- Function → Funktion
- Variable → Variable

**Question Formats:**
- Multiple Choice → Multiple Choice / Mehrfachauswahl
- True/False → Richtig/Falsch or Wahr/Falsch
- Fill in the blank → Lückentext
- Short answer → Kurzantwort
