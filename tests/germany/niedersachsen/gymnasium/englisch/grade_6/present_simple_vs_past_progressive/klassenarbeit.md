## üí° Grammatik-Hilfe

### Present Simple
- **Verwendung:** Gewohnheiten, Routinen, allgemeine Fakten
- **Signalw√∂rter:** always, usually, every day, never, often, sometimes
- **Bildung:** 
  - I/you/we/they + Verb
  - he/she/it + Verb**-s** ‚ö†Ô∏è
- **Negation:** don't/doesn't + Verb
- **Frage:** Do/Does + Subjekt + Verb?

### Past Progressive  
- **Verwendung:** Handlungen, die zu einem Zeitpunkt in der Vergangenheit abliefen
- **Signalw√∂rter:** while, when, at that moment, at 5 pm yesterday
- **Bildung:** was/were + Verb**-ing**
  - I/he/she/it + **was** + Verb-ing
  - you/we/they + **were** + Verb-ing
- **Negation:** wasn't/weren't + Verb-ing
- **Frage:** Was/Were + Subjekt + Verb-ing?
