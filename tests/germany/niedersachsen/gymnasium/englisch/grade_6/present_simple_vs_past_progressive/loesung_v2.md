## Teil 5: Creative Writing (5 Punkte)

**Bewertungskriterien:**
- Grammatik (2 Punkte): Korrekte Past Progressive Verwendung
- Inhalt (2 Punkte): Mindestens 3× Past Progressive
- Verständlichkeit (1 Punkt): Klare Sätze

**Beispiellösungen:**
1. At 4 pm yesterday, I was doing my homework.
2. My brother was playing video games.
3. While I was studying, my mum was cooking dinner.
4. The sun was shining and the birds were singing.
5. I was feeling happy and relaxed.
